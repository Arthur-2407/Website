Changed the concurrency model of the Flask/SocketIO Face AI microservice from 'eventlet' (with monkey patching) to standard multi-threading ('threading'). Eventlet's monkey patching of standard library threads/sockets is known to cause severe deadlocks when combined with native C++ multi-threaded deep learning libraries (TensorFlow/RetinaFace/PyTorch/OpenCV), causing the service to freeze/hang indefinitely on curl health probes and POST requests.

CHANGES:
- Removed eventlet import and monkey_patch() in face-ai-service/src/main.py.
